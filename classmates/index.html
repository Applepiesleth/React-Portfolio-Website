A project developed during a 48-hour hackathon for the class CMPUT 401 at the University of Alberta. This webapp allows you to find other students that share similar classes with you, giving a percent ranking of how close your schedules match! The app allows you to upload your calender ics file obtainable from the University of Alberta student dashboard.

For this app, I worked primarily in the frontend, utilizing React and the MUI library.
