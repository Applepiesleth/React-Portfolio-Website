Fairness Farm

Aug 2022
Play Game
Fairness Farm is a game aimed to teach the general public and children concepts of fairness in an AI context.

The game was developed at the TRAM Lab at the University of Alberta, along with Mira Patel, supervised by Nidhi Hegde.
Unity

Details

The game features Market 1, which teaches the player concepts of Fairness in the AI context through 7 levels. This is followed by a quiz to test the player's understanding, which leads to Market 2. Market 2 challenges the player to balance fairness with a primary goal, mimicking the challenges of achieving fairness while having an accurate AI system.
