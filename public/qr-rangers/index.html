Details

QR Rangers was a project for the class CMPUT 301 at the University of Alberta. It is an Android application for collecting QR codes in order to gain points. Users are able to keep a gallery of their collected QR codes. There are features such as leaderboards and a map of collected QR codes, with the information stored in a Google Firebase database.
