Details

Phoebe and Demi Bot is a Discord bot intended to be used in the server of my webcomic. The bot is named after robot characters found in the series. It is programmed using the discord.py API, and is hosted on a Cybera cloud server.

Phoebe and Demi have basic server management features, such as welcome messages, and role choice options. It also has unique commands that present users with conversation topics, quotes from the webcomic, and even works as a magic 8-ball! It also used to have the feature for staging announcements to be sent at a later time, but is currently removed due to issues with accidental message posting.
