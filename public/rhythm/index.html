Play Game
Beating enemies to the beat is no fun as a solo performance. Orchestrate a quartet of heroes and harmoniously defeat the incoming onslaught of enemies!

Made for the "Let's Jam Together!" game jam.
Unity
Aseprite

Details

This game was made in a team of 3, with me focusing on the art of the game.

"There's no I in Rhythm" is, obviously, a rhythm game, in which the song gets faster and faster the more you go on. You take control of 4 heroes, Juniper, Gunther, Diana, and Angus, as they defeat the enemies in their way to the rhythm. The game was also developed to not have any text in it.
